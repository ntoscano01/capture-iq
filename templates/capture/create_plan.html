About Capture Plans

A capture plan tracks a high-value sales opportunity from initial discovery through proposal submission and award.

Start Date: When you first learn about the opportunity (pre-release).

Stages:

  • Pre-Release: Before RFP released, opportunity identified
  • RFP Released: Official solicitation published
  • Proposal Submitted: Your team's response submitted
  • Won/Lost: Contract awarded or opportunity closed

You can edit all details after creation, and add team members to share the capture plan.
